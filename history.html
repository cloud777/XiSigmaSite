History of the Xi Sigma Chapter

The Xi Sigma Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc. was founded with a mission to uplift, inspire, and lead. Since its inception, our chapter has continued to make an impact both on and off campus through service, leadership, and brotherhood.

The brothers of Xi Sigma have upheld the four cardinal principles: Manhood, Scholarship, Perseverance, and Uplift. Our history is built on a legacy of excellence, community engagement, and academic achievement.

From campus events to community service initiatives, Xi Sigma continues to be a guiding light of leadership and purpose. Our alumni have gone on to succeed in diverse fields and remain committed to mentoring the next generation of brothers.
